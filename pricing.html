FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Can I cancel anytime?▼
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. No questions asked, no penalties. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.
What are AI tokens used for?▼
AI tokens power advanced features like the Transformation AI (body prediction), custom meal plan generation, and detailed supplement analysis reports. Pro users get 1,000 tokens/month; Elite users get unlimited.
Is the AI actually personalized?▼
Yes. All recommendations are generated based on your profile — age, weight, height, goal, body type, and activity level. The AI learns from your logs and adjusts over time.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?▼
Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial for paid plans?▼
The Free plan gives you full access to core features permanently. We occasionally offer 7-day Pro trials — sign up to check current promotions.
